Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional fillings: cheese, herbs, vegetables, or cooked meats

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs, tilting the pan to spread them evenly.
  4. Cook until the edges set, then add fillings if desired.
  5. Fold the omelette in half and slide onto a plate.
